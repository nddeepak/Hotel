Welcome to ABC Hotel

Hotel ABC established in 1980 is one of the finest star hotel at Coimbatore.Our Hotel is recognised for its comfortable facilities and providing a homely environment for its customers.ABC has been awarded as the "best Hotel of the year" by the TamilNadu State government in 2010.The hotel comprises of 25 floors with 485 rooms ,5 Conference halls and 2 party halls. To provide best services the hotel provides facilities such as shops,cinema theater,gym,restaurant,internet cafe,swimming pools,medical aid,library,playstations,ATM etc.
